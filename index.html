Who am I?

I'm Anna, a self-taught programmer, who loves building things with code. I learnt my first programming language - Visual Basic - at the beginning of my professional career and ever since I discovered a true passion for automating repetitive tasks by simply writing code in spreadsheets. But then, when Covid hit suddenly and I had more time to learn, I started looking into other technologies that could help me increase the efficiency of various internal business processes within my team. This way I found myself learning the basics of Python and turned tasks that had taken previously hours into a few mouse clicks.

However, at the beginning of 2021 I decided to fully dive into the world of coding by learning other powerful technologies. I'm now seeking a full-time role where I can contribute my skills both in coding and business.
